# Mathematics - 2025/26

## Exam:

There will be a written exam consisting of both theoretical questions
and practical problems to solve. The maximum points from the exam is 50.
In addition, there is possibility to obtain 10 bonus points from
homework (5 assignments, each is 2 points). To pass the exam you need 25
points of the total 60.
